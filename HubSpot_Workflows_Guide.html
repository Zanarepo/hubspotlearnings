Essential Workflows

Marketing

Lead Nurturing

Educate a lead and transition them seamlessly down the sales funnel.

  • ⚡
    Trigger: Form submission (eBook / Whitepaper)
  • 1
    Send immediate "Thank You & Download" Email
    • Wait 3 Days
  • 2
    Send follow-up educational email
  • ?
    Condition: Clicked a link in 2nd email?
    YES Increase Lead Score & Assign to Sales
    NO Send secondary resource email
Marketing Service

Welcome & Onboarding

Greet new users and provide immediate value to reduce churn.

  • ⚡
    Trigger: New Subscriber or Free Trial Signup
  • 1
    Send "Welcome" email with top resources
    • Wait 2 Days
  • 2
    Send "Pro-Tips" highlighting core features
    • Wait 3 Days
  • 3
    Send personal check-in or webinar invite
Sales

Internal Lead Routing

Ensure high-value, hot leads are followed up with instantly.

  • ⚡
    Trigger: Lead Score > 50 OR Views Pricing page 2+ times
  • 1
    Assign Contact to Rep (Round-robin logic)
  • 2
    Create "Call Lead" Task in CRM (Due in 24h)
  • 3
    Push notification to Rep via Slack/Email integration
Marketing

Wake Up Campaign

Purge the database and optionally re-engage completely cold contacts.

  • ⚡
    Trigger: No opens or web visits in 6 months
  • 1
    Send "We miss you" special offer email
    • Wait 7 Days
  • ?
    Condition: Clicked or Opened?
    YES Pull out of list, mark active
    NO Mark as Disqualified / Unsubscribe
Sales Service

Deal Stage Pipeline

Run administrative handover tasks when a deal is closed won.

  • ⚡
    Trigger: Deal stage shifts to "Closed Won"
  • 1
    Send automated "Next Steps" handoff to Client
  • 2
    Generate Onboarding Ticket in Service Hub Pipeline
  • 3
    Alert Customer Success Manager to begin onboarding
Service

NPS / Satisfaction

Measure sentiment right after a positive or negative interaction.

  • ⚡
    Trigger: Support ticket moved to "Closed"
    • Wait 24 Hours
  • 1
    Dispatch CSAT or NPS Survey via Email
  • ?
    Condition: Is Score <= 6 (A Detractor)?
    YES Create urgent task for CS Rep to reach out & resolve
